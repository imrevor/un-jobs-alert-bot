# UN Jobs Alert Bot

Free Telegram bot that sends you UN and international development job alerts based on your filters.

**[Start the Bot →](https://t.me/UNJobsAlertBot)**

## Features

- Filter by location, organization, grade, and sector
- Automatic alerts every 4 hours
- Covers unjobs.org and impactpool.org
- P-1 to D-2, consultancies, internships, UNV, and more
- 100% free

## How It Works

1. Open [@UNJobsAlertBot](https://t.me/UNJobsAlertBot) on Telegram
2. Set your filters (location, org, grade, sector)
3. Get automatic alerts when new jobs match
